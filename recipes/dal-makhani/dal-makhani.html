Dal Makhani

What is Dal Makhani

Creamy & buttery Dal Makhani is one of India's most special and popular dal. Black lentils are cooked with lot of butter and cream making it a special dish.

Ingredients needed

  • 3/4 cup urad dal
  • 1/4 cup red kidney beans (rajmah)
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 8 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 2 inches gingerroot (chopped)
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 large tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • methi
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • salt

Directions

  1. Soak the black urad and beans overnight in 3 to 4 cups of water
  2. Cook the soaked dal and beans in the same water with salt, red chili powder and half the ginger until the dal and beans are cooked and soft
  3. Peel and chop the onion, ginger, garlic and tomatoes
  4. Heat the oil and butter in a pan, add cumin seeds and once they start crackling, throw in the chopped onions and cook until golden brown
  5. Now add the chopped ginger, garlic and tomatoes to the golden brown onions. Saute until tomatoes are mashed, and then add the boiled dal and beans, without the liquid
  6. Mash the dals to get a nice creamy texture
  7. Now add the liquid (and water if necessary) and let simmer on very low heat for 15 minutes
  8. After 15 minutes, add the fresh cream and garam masala powder, and continue letting simmer for another 5 minutes
  9. Finish off with some methi on top
  10. Serve with Naan or roti and enjoy!
